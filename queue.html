WHAT IS QUEUE?
Queue is a second screen application designed to enhance and improve student-professor communication in large classroom environments. Queue allows for students to anonymously and collectively ask their professors questions. The instructor receives notification of classroom discussions on their device and may choose to either answer questions as they arise, save them for the end, or to go over in more depth later.
